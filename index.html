AgroShield supports farmers without smartphones. Scan a disease on this device, go to the Share tab, and send an SMS from the SMS Alert section. Any basic mobile phone can receive the formatted disease report — no internet required on their end.

🤖 About the AI

Disease detection is powered by Anthropic's Claude Vision AI — one of the most accurate multimodal models available. The system rejects non-plant images, identifies the crop type, disease name, pathogen, severity, and generates customized treatment and prevention plans.
